01 / About

Backend by trade, systems by instinct.

I'm an Associate Technical Lead and backend engineer who cares more about how a system behaves at 3am under real load than how it looks in a demo. My work sits at the intersection of API design, data modelling, and infrastructure — designing service boundaries, tuning databases, and building the pipelines that move data between them.

Over the last few years I've moved from shipping features to owning architecture: leading currency-transfer backend systems, migrating analytics pipelines to ClickHouse with zero data loss, and mentoring engineers on service design. My current focus is distributed systems, system design, and performance engineering — the kind of problems where the right abstraction saves everyone a very bad night.
